Information Technology and Communication, ITC

The role of technology is to make operations easier and to accelerate operations and consequently produce prompt results. Technology also improves the capacities and capabilities of the human resource deployed
A majority of DIL’s business and activities are carried out online. The entire recruitment process except for the interviews is done online. It then follows that the greatest investment has gone into developing a robust website and database infrastructure.

Intelligent Application Development

Data on applicants and job orders is collected responsibly and intelligently. The application enables data processing and data analysis so as to draw business lessons on the nature of jobs available as well as being able to qualitatively assess the talent pool. The following application was developed: A website that integrates both the recruitment software as well as the candidates’ database. The site is also the online page for anyone seeking information about DIL

Accounting and Finance

The software program was developed for the entry of data from financial transactions as well as for the generation of reports.

Communication

Emails, phone systems and intranets set up for efficient communication within the company are seamlessly and efficiently linked.

Data Mining Tools

Data analysis is the lifeblood of the company. DIL thrives from data collection to determine the strategic business direction.

Modus Operandi for Contracted Jobs

Depending on the tasks to be accomplished, DIL has a dedicated team of professionals embedded to the Client Project Manager for efficient execution of tasks at hand and advance planning. The Company coordinates and activates all the relevant cells within their data base for immediate deployment if need be within 24 hour notice and handles all the logistics pertaining to following:
  1. Transportation of the workers to and from place of abode.
  2. Arrange for their meals and accommodation.
  3. Pay for their Medical Insurance and Statutory Government deductions.
  4. Provide all the safety gear and tools while at work or make arrangement with the client for such provisions at a cost.
  5. Ensure all HSE protocols are adhered to as per Public Health Regulations
  6. Enforce and ensure discipline and good work ethics are maintained at all times

Challenges and pain points that DIL resolves for Clients

  1. Assist upcoming businesses with start-up technical and logistical support
  2. The start-up company to operate with a lean manageable staff on core business
  3. We source for the personnel and manage them on behalf of the company
  4. Conduct training and deploy accordingly on behalf of the company
  5. Reduce the Client Company’s operating expenses by offloading the burden of the following incidental cost:
    • Daily transport to and from work
    • Cost of feeding and accommodation allowances
    • Offset cost of monthly statutory contributions to the exchequer (NHIF, NSSF and PAYE)
    • No monthly pension contribution to the workers.
    • No Final lump sum and subsequent monthly pension payment to the workers on retirement
    • HR Department to manage a leaner Staff as compared to a large workforce
    • The company will never lose working days in terms of leave, indiscipline and sick-offs.
    • Less expenses on Staff Welfare in terms of car, home, school and emergency loans.
    • Less expenses on insurance cover for bloated workforce.

Anti-terrorism Course

Target Group: All security personnel, government and private administrators, Service Industry Managers, legislators, Media teams and all individuals working in terror attack prone places. The ATAP course has been designed to provide attendees, with an understanding of the history of terrorism, and contribute to counteraction and reduction of the current threat. The program covers:
  • Definitions & types of terrorism
  • An overview Kenyan experience of terrorism
  • Terrorist trend & Interrelationship with other crimes
  • Tactics and targets
  • Counter terrorism organizations
  • Terrorist attack cycle (TAPI)
  • Situational awareness
  • Introduction to terrorism surveillance ops
  • Introduction to terrorism investigations
  • Overview legal aspects
  • Hostage taking and kidnapping
  • Improvised explosive device & favorite weapons of use awareness
  • Post attack & incident considerations
  • Terrorism and the media
  • Terrorism Financing
  • Human Rights vs Counter Terrorism Ops
  • Contemporary Threats

Physical Security Training Program

The physical security training program is designed to provide baseline knowledge of physical security systems and procedures. The survey process is the common thread used in teaching this program. The PSTP provides the participants with fundamental knowledge on many relevant physical security issues, which include vulnerability assessment, familiarization with hardware and procedures. A comprehensive practical exercise is followed by a formal presentation of the survey results by each of the survey groups at the end. Target Group: Security Agents/ Managers responsible for protection of vital installations including hotels, office blocks, shopping malls, schools and colleges, warehouses, factories and other large installations.
