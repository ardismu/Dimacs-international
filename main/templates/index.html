DIMACS International Ltd.

'Kuna mwanga mbele'


WHO ARE WE?

Dimacs International Ltd. is a limited liability Company incorporated under the companies Act Chapter 486 of the Laws of Kenya. The company is owned by a group of Senior Retired Military Officers from the three branches of Kenya Defence Forces namely, The Kenya Army, Kenya Air Force and Kenya Navy. Other members of the disciplined forces like the Kenya National Police, Kenya Wildlife Services, Kenya Forest Services and other Government Security related agencies have also joined in to become members. The company was registered after the realization of the vast wasted knowledge and talent after retirement of disciplined forces personnel. Generally in the Defence Forces, people retire on a fixed-age limit pegged on a rank-to-age fixed timeframe. This has resulted in an ever growing “Pool of Young Retired Professionals” who are between early 40s up to 60 years. Most of the retirees have had sterling careers spanning over twenty years in the service after intensive training both locally and abroad. This brain wastage has resulted in draining the government resources on training people who it expends with before maximum utilization of their professional resources. Unfortunately it creates a pool of idle but highly professional and reliable people. Suffice to say that this highly vulnerable group can easily be influenced and manipulated to engage in anti-social activities if their future welfare is not well articulated after retirement. In essence, this group is both a ticking time bomb and a goldmine. Dimacs International Ltd. wants to treat it as the latter.

MESL Guards Company Profile

INTRODUCTION

Marine Escort Services Limited (MESL) is a limited liability company incorporated under the Companies Act Chapter 486 of the laws of Kenya. The company is owned by a group of senior retired military officers, and members from other disciplined forces of the Kenyan government. As such, MESL boasts of the vastness of its pool of professional expertise to ensure unparalleled service in the security industry.

OUR VISION

To provide reliable integrated security solutions with unparalleled service, and quality delivery on land, air, and sea.

OUR MISSION

To develop a global footprint with the capacity to deploy diligent and efficient teams globally to secure and support Land, Air, and Maritime operations

WHY MESL?

MESL has a countrywide liaison and network outreach that can enable our clientele to operate with the authority of our assurance on their safety and security.

MODE OF OPERATION

Private-Public Partnerships: The company has programmed to develop Institutional Public-Private Partnerships in the management of Integrated Security Solutions by building synergies with other Government Agencies. To this end, MESL sought and was granted authority to operate Armed Marine Escort Guards vide letter SEC.PO.L 22/12/5/(59) Dated 20th January 2011 by The Ministry of Interior & Coordination of National Government.
  1. Regional Coordinators and County Commissioners-: To facilitate easy integration and cooperation with Government Security Agents especially in the North Eastern Region which is prone to terrorist attacks.
  2. The Kenya Firearms Bureau-: Facilitate in issuing Import/Export and Temporary Firearm Licenses well before the ship arrives at the port for the sake of verification.
  3. The Immigration-: Issue Temporary Permits and visas for the visiting and transit crew onboard, well before the ship's arrival and subsequent docking.
  4. The Kenya Police Service-: Safe storage of weapons in the Police Armoury at the Kilindini Port and law enforcement where there is culpability by a visiting Vessel.
  5. The KRA and Customs Department:- Verification of weapons on board the vessel and enforcement of due payments to be made to the exchequer.
  6. The Kenya Maritime Authority – Obtain information on all incoming Merchant Ships with Armed Guards Onboard through their local Agents

  1. Provision of secure transportation and Courier Services:-

    2. Bulk Containers escort and eventual delivery to the owner or a secure storage
  2. Provision of Security on Business, Private Premises, and Infrastructure:-
    1. We provide highly trained Guards with military background to secure your premises whether it’s a residential, commercial building or High Value Mobile or Static Structures.
    2. We have a specialists’ team who have previous experience in Kenya’s military infrastructure development wing and hence can double up as Security Foremen in any type of construction.
  3. Provision of VIP Escort and Protection Assignments:- MESL customizes the needs of an individual or a group of people when it comes to VIP Protection and Escort Services. We have a pool of well-trained team and some of them are licensed gun holders for such duties.
  4. Provision Of Intelligence Services for Criminal Activities in an Organization:- MESL has a wide Network of retired personnel who have worked in the government security sector. Depending on the nature of the Crime to be investigated, MESL can provide the necessary experts with requisite knowledge of the type of crime to be investigated. Whether it’s simple theft, pilferage, or cybercrime; all types of criminal activities in an Organization.
  5. Provision of Security on Organized Events:- Do you intend to have a Political Rally in an uncharted grounds? What about a Wedding, Funeral ceremony, Open Air Religious gathering or Celebratory Party? Please contact MESL for an unforgettable serene and conducive secure environment organized by MESL Out-door Security Organizers.
